About Homing Dove International

Homing Dove International South Sudan is a multifaceted organization committed to providing quality education, financial services, and essential commodities to the people of South Sudan.

Founded in 2010, we have grown to become a trusted name in education with our Nursery, Primary, and Secondary schools, as well as a reliable provider of financial services including money transfer and loans.

Loan Application

Loan Requirements

  • Valid National ID or Passport
  • Proof of Income (for employed applicants)
  • Business Registration (for business loans)
  • Two recent passport photos
  • Collateral may be required for larger loans

Interest Rates

Our interest rates are competitive and vary based on loan type and duration:

  • Personal Loans: 10-15% annually
  • Business Loans: 8-12% annually
  • Education Loans: 5-8% annually
